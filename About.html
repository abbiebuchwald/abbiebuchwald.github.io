Hi, I'm Abbie Buchwald

I'm a Boston-based graphic designer.

I grew up in Colorado and North Carolina, and have studied at Gordon College and Lesley University. My parents knew I would go into design before I did; my mom always tells this story about me drawing a wooden model as a ballerina at a children's area in an art museum when I was about five years old. A worker came up to my mother and said, “I have never seen a child so focused on what they are making”. I have an extensive background in visual art, and I strive to implement my various areas of expertise into my work.

Clients & Experience:

GLBTQ+ Legal Advocates & Defenders (GLAD) - Graphic Design & Multimedia Intern
